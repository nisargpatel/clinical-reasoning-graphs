### Leading diagnosis (with probability)
**Herpesvirus necrotizing retinitis (VZV > HSV), i.e., ARN/PORN in the setting of profound iatrogenic cellular immunosuppression** — **45%**

### Reasoning
- **Ocular phenotype fits necrotizing herpetic retinitis:** rapid progression to **light perception only** in the right eye, **panuveitis**, vitreous inflammation (3+ cells/haze), and **circumferential yellow retinal lesions** described as “consistent with infection.” This pattern is classic for **VZV/HSV necrotizing retinitis** (ARN), and in patients with **very low CD4 counts** it can look like **PORN**.
- **Host risk is extremely high:** she developed **profound T-cell lymphopenia (CD4 46/mm³, CD8 97/mm³)** after intensive immunosuppression (high-dose glucocorticoids, rituximab, cyclophosphamide, biologics). That degree of cellular immunodeficiency strongly predisposes to **VZV/HSV/CMV retinitis**.
- **CNS findings can accompany VZV/HSV:** severe headaches plus CSF **pleocytosis with high protein** can occur with **VZV meningitis/vasculopathy**; neutrophilic predominance can be seen early.
- **Negative viral PCRs do not fully exclude it:** PCR sensitivity can drop after **systemic valganciclovir** and **intravitreal antivirals**, and sampling/compartment issues (vitreous vs retina vs aqueous) can yield false negatives.

Behçet remains on the list because of the long history of **recurrent oral ulcers, skin lesions, and inflammatory eye disease** with **HLA-B51 positivity**, but the *current* retinal picture and profound CD4 depletion strongly push toward an **opportunistic necrotizing retinitis** process.

- **Intraocular lymphoma / masquerade syndrome** (especially with steroid exposure) can mimic uveitis/retinitis and can be missed if one assumes infection or Behçet.
